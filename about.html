About Silk Reads

Here at Silk Reads you can use the google books api to search up any book your heart desires. You can search by either the books title or the books author and will be provided with information on the book along with a link to purchase. You will also be able to see books related to your initial search with clickable titles and authors to search up whatever was clicked on.

The name Silk Reads is a play on the historic trade route the Silk Road. This was a network of Euroasian trade routes active from the second century BCE until the mid-15th century. Just like the Silk Road was used to share knowledge between the East and the West, Silk Reads shares knowledge between book enthusiasts across the world.
